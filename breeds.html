The Maine Coon is the largest breed of domestic cat. On average, males weigh from 5.9 to 8.2 kg. The body is solid and muscular, which is necessary for supporting their own weight, and the chest is broad. Equally at home with children, dogs, or older persons, the Maine Coon is an ideal pet, handily earning its status as the second most popular breed in America, as well as it's fond nickname, "The Gentle Giant."
